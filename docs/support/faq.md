## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit our website at [crmplatform.my](https://crmplatform.my)
2. Click "Start Free Trial" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile setup

Your account includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all features.

### What browsers are supported?

We support the latest two versions of:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Apple Safari

For the best experience, we recommend using Google Chrome with JavaScript enabled.

### Is there a mobile app?

Currently, CRM Platform is optimized for web browsers on both desktop and mobile devices. Our responsive design ensures a great experience on tablets and smartphones. A dedicated mobile app is on our roadmap for future development.

### How do I invite team members?

1. Go to **Settings > Team Management**
2. Click **Invite User**
3. Enter the team member's email address
4. Select their role (Admin, Manager, Sales Rep, or Viewer)
5. Click **Send Invitation**

The invited user will receive an email with instructions to join your organization.

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for the reset link (check spam folder if not received)
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

Password reset links expire after 24 hours.

### How do I change my email address?

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**
2. Click **Edit** next to your email
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify the new email by clicking the confirmation link
5. Your email will be updated once verified

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

We highly recommend enabling 2FA for enhanced account security.

### Can I have multiple users under one account?

Yes! Our subscription plans support multiple users:
- **Free**: 1 user
- **Professional**: Up to 5 users
- **Business**: Up to 25 users
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited users

Additional users can be added from **Settings > Team Management**.

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:
1. Go to **Settings > Account**
2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Click **Delete Account**

**Important**: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted after 30 days. You can export your data before deletion.

## Lead Management

### What is a lead?

A lead is a potential customer who has shown interest in your products or services. Leads are typically generated from website forms, marketing campaigns, referrals, or manual entry.

### How do I create a new lead?

1. Go to **Leads** from the main menu
2. Click **+ New Lead**
3. Fill in the lead details:
   - Company name
   - Contact name and email
   - Source (website, referral, etc.)
   - Notes
4. Click **Save**

### What is lead scoring?

Lead scoring is a points-based system that helps prioritize leads based on their likelihood to convert. Scores are calculated based on:
- Profile fit (company size, industry, budget)
- Engagement level (email opens, website visits)
- Behavior signals (demo requests, pricing page views)

Higher scores indicate more qualified leads.

### How do I qualify or disqualify a lead?

1. Open the lead details
2. Click **Qualify** or **Disqualify**
3. For qualification: The lead will be marked as qualified
4. For disqualification: Select a reason and add notes

Qualified leads can then be converted to opportunities.

### How do I convert a lead to an opportunity?

1. Open a qualified lead
2. Click **Convert to Opportunity**
3. Fill in opportunity details (value, pipeline stage, etc.)
4. Optionally create a new customer record
5. Click **Convert**

The lead will be marked as converted and linked to the new opportunity.

### Can I import leads in bulk?

Yes! You can import leads from CSV or Excel files:
1. Go to **Leads > Import**
2. Download the template file
3. Fill in your lead data
4. Upload the completed file
5. Map columns to CRM fields
6. Review and confirm import

Maximum 10,000 leads per import.

## Customer Management

### How do I add a new customer?

1. Go to **Customers** from the main menu
2. Click **+ New Customer**
3. Fill in customer details:
   - Company name and code
   - Contact information
   - Address
   - Industry and type
4. Click **Save**

### What's the difference between a lead and a customer?

- **Lead**: A potential customer who hasn't purchased yet
- **Customer**: An active customer who has made a purchase or signed a contract

Leads become customers when they convert through your sales pipeline.

### How do I add contacts to a customer?

1. Open the customer record
2. Go to the **Contacts** tab
3. Click **+ Add Contact**
4. Enter contact details (name, email, phone, role)
5. Set as primary contact if applicable
6. Click **Save**

You can add unlimited contacts per customer.

### Can I merge duplicate customers?

Yes! To merge duplicate records:
1. Go to **Customers > Duplicates**
2. Review potential duplicates
3. Select the records to merge
4. Choose the primary record to keep
5. Click **Merge**

Data from both records will be consolidated into the primary record.

### How do I export customer data?

1. Go to **Customers**
2. Apply any filters if needed
3. Click **Export**
4. Select format (CSV, Excel, or JSON)
5. Choose fields to export
6. Click **Download**

## Sales Pipeline

### What is a sales pipeline?

A sales pipeline is a visual representation of your sales process, showing deals at different stages from initial contact to closed won/lost. It helps you track progress and forecast revenue.

### How do I create a new pipeline?

1. Go to **Settings > Pipelines**
2. Click **+ New Pipeline**
3. Name your pipeline
4. Add stages (e.g., Qualification, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won)
5. Set probability percentages for each stage
6. Click **Save**

### How do I move a deal between stages?

You can move deals in two ways:

**Kanban Board:**
1. Go to the Pipeline view
2. Drag and drop the deal card to the desired stage

**Deal Details:**
1. Open the deal
2. Click **Move Stage**
3. Select the new stage
4. Add notes if needed

### What is weighted pipeline value?

Weighted pipeline value = Deal Value Ã— Stage Probability

For example: A $10,000 deal at 50% probability stage = $5,000 weighted value

This helps forecast expected revenue based on likelihood of closing.

### How do I close a deal as won or lost?

1. Open the deal or opportunity
2. Click **Mark as Won** or **Mark as Lost**
3. For won: Enter actual close date and value
4. For lost: Select a reason and add notes
5. Click **Confirm**

### How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment details if not on file
5. Click **Upgrade**

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged the prorated difference.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select a cancellation reason
4. Confirm cancellation

Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing period. After cancellation, you can still access your data in read-only mode for 30 days.

### What payment methods are accepted?

We accept:
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- FPX (Online Banking Malaysia)
- E-wallets (GrabPay, Touch 'n Go, Boost)
- Bank transfer (for annual plans)

### How do I get an invoice?

Invoices are automatically generated for each payment:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Invoices**
2. Click on any invoice to view details
3. Click **Download PDF** to get a copy

You can also set up automatic invoice emails in billing settings.

### Is there a refund policy?

- Monthly plans: Pro-rata refund within 14 days
- Annual plans: Pro-rata refund within 30 days
- No refunds after the refund period

Contact support@crmplatform.my for refund requests.

## Security & Privacy

### How is my data protected?

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: TLS 1.2+ for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Authentication**: Password hashing with bcrypt, optional 2FA
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring
- **Compliance**: PDPA (Malaysia) and GDPR compliant

### Where is my data stored?

Primary data is stored in secure data centers in Malaysia, with backup facilities in Singapore. All data centers are ISO 27001 certified.

### Can I export all my data?

Yes! You have the right to data portability:
1. Go to **Settings > Privacy**
2. Click **Export My Data**
3. Select data types to export
4. Choose format (JSON or CSV)
5. Click **Request Export**

You'll receive a download link via email within 24 hours.

### How do I request data deletion?

To request complete data deletion:
1. Go to **Settings > Privacy**
2. Click **Delete My Data**
3. Confirm your request

All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance.

### Who can access my data?

Only users within your organization can access your data, based on their assigned roles:
- **Admin**: Full access to all data
- **Manager**: Access to team data
- **Sales Rep**: Access to own data and assigned records
- **Viewer**: Read-only access

Our support team may access data for troubleshooting with your permission.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in to my account

Try these steps:
1. Check your email and password are correct
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Reset your password
5. Check if your account is suspended

If issues persist, contact support@crmplatform.my.

### The page is loading slowly

Try these solutions:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Try incognito/private mode

Check our [status page](/status) for any ongoing issues.

### I'm not receiving emails from CRM Platform

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add no-reply@crmplatform.my to your contacts
3. Check email filters and rules
4. Verify your email address in settings
5. Contact support if issues persist

### Data isn't syncing correctly

1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Log out and log back in
4. Clear browser cache
5. Check for any error messages

Report persistent sync issues to support with details of what's not syncing.

### How do I report a bug?

1. Go to **Help > Report a Bug**
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. Include steps to reproduce
4. Attach screenshots if possible
5. Submit the report

You can also email bugs@crmplatform.my directly.
