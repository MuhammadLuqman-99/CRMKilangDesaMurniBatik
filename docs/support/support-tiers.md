## Tier Details

### Free Tier

**Best for**: Individual users and small teams exploring CRM Platform.

**Includes**:
- Self-service knowledge base access
- Community forum participation
- Email support (48-hour response)
- Basic troubleshooting guides

**Support Hours**: Email responses during business hours only.

---

### Professional Tier

**Best for**: Growing teams who need reliable support.

**Includes**:
- Everything in Free tier
- Live chat support during business hours
- 24-hour email response guarantee
- Priority email routing
- Access to video tutorials
- Monthly product webinars

**Support Hours**:
- Live Chat: 9 AM - 6 PM MYT (Mon-Fri)
- Email: 24-hour response guarantee

---

### Business Tier

**Best for**: Established businesses requiring fast, comprehensive support.

**Includes**:
- Everything in Professional tier
- Phone support
- 8-hour response guarantee
- Priority support queue
- Quarterly training sessions (1 per quarter)
- Technical implementation guidance
- API support

**Support Hours**:
- Phone: 8 AM - 8 PM MYT (Mon-Sat)
- Live Chat: Extended hours
- Email: 8-hour response guarantee

**Phone Support**: +60 3-XXXX XXXX

---

### Enterprise Tier

**Best for**: Large organizations with complex requirements.

**Includes**:
- Everything in Business tier
- 4-hour response guarantee
- Dedicated support agent
- Named account manager
- Unlimited training sessions
- Custom onboarding program
- 24/7 emergency support
- Quarterly business reviews
- Custom SLA options
- Direct Slack/Teams channel

**Support Hours**: 24/7/365 for critical issues

**Emergency Hotline**: Contact your account manager

## Response Time Definitions

| Priority | Description | Free | Pro | Business | Enterprise |
|----------|-------------|------|-----|----------|------------|
| **P1 - Critical** | System down, data loss | 48h | 24h | 4h | 1h |
| **P2 - High** | Major feature broken | 48h | 24h | 8h | 2h |
| **P3 - Medium** | Feature issue, workaround exists | 48h | 24h | 24h | 8h |
| **P4 - Low** | Questions, minor issues | 48h | 48h | 48h | 24h |

**Response Time** = Time from ticket submission to first meaningful response.

---

## Priority Definitions

### P1 - Critical
- Complete system outage
- Data loss or corruption
- Security breach
- All users unable to work

### P2 - High
- Major feature not working
- Significant performance degradation
- Multiple users affected
- No workaround available

### P3 - Medium
- Feature partially working
- Single user affected
- Workaround available
- Non-urgent issues

### P4 - Low
- How-to questions
- Feature requests
- Minor UI issues
- General inquiries

## Escalation Process

If you're not satisfied with your support experience:

1. **Level 1**: Request escalation in your ticket
2. **Level 2**: Email escalation@crmplatform.my
3. **Level 3**: Contact your account manager (Enterprise)
4. **Level 4**: Executive escalation (available for Enterprise on critical issues)

## Support Best Practices

### Get Faster Resolutions

1. **Be specific**: Include exact error messages
2. **Provide context**: What were you trying to do?
3. **Include screenshots**: Visual evidence helps
4. **List steps to reproduce**: Help us see what you see
5. **Note your browser/device**: Technical details matter

### What to Include in Tickets

```
Subject: [Brief description of issue]

Account Email: your@email.com
Company: Your Company Name
Plan: Professional/Business/Enterprise

Issue Description:
[Detailed description of the problem]

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to...
2. Click on...
3. See error...

Expected Behavior:
[What should happen]

Actual Behavior:
[What actually happens]

Screenshots:
[Attach relevant screenshots]

Browser/Device:
Chrome 120 / Windows 11
```

## Upgrading Support

To upgrade to a higher support tier:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your desired plan
4. Complete the upgrade

Or contact sales@crmplatform.my for Enterprise inquiries.
