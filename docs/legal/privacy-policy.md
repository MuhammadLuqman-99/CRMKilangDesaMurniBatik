## 5. Data Retention Policy

### 5.1 Active Accounts

While your account is active:
- Account data: Retained throughout subscription
- Customer data: Retained throughout subscription
- Usage logs: 2 years
- Security logs: 3 years

### 5.2 After Account Termination

- Customer data: Deleted after 30 days (or exported upon request)
- Account data: Deleted after 90 days
- Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Anonymized analytics: Retained indefinitely

### 5.3 Data Deletion Requests

Upon request, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except:
- Data required for legal compliance
- Data needed to resolve disputes
- Anonymized/aggregated data

## 6. User Rights

### 6.1 Right to Access

You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Know what data we hold about you
- Understand how your data is processed

### 6.2 Right to Rectification

You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Complete incomplete data
- Update outdated information

### 6.3 Right to Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data when:
- Data is no longer necessary for the purpose collected
- You withdraw consent
- Data was unlawfully processed

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to:
- Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON, CSV)
- Transfer your data to another service provider
- Request direct transfer where technically feasible

### 6.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to limit processing when:
- Accuracy of data is contested
- Processing is unlawful
- We no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims

### 6.6 Right to Object

You have the right to object to:
- Processing based on legitimate interests
- Direct marketing
- Profiling

### 6.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision Making

You have the right to:
- Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing
- Request human intervention
- Challenge automated decisions

### 6.8 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights:

1. **Email:** privacy@crmplatform.my
2. **In-App:** Settings > Privacy > Data Rights
3. **Written Request:** Send to our registered address

We will respond within:
- PDPA: 21 days
- GDPR: 30 days

## 13. PDPA (Malaysia) Compliance

### 13.1 Seven Principles

We comply with the seven principles of PDPA:

1. **General Principle:** Consent obtained for data processing
2. **Notice and Choice:** Clear notice provided about data use
3. **Disclosure:** Data disclosed only as permitted
4. **Security:** Appropriate safeguards implemented
5. **Retention:** Data retained only as necessary
6. **Data Integrity:** Data kept accurate and complete
7. **Access:** Users can access and correct their data

### 13.2 Sensitive Personal Data

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (health, religious beliefs, political opinions, etc.) unless specifically required and with explicit consent.

### 13.3 Complaints

If you have concerns about our data practices, you may:
1. Contact our Data Protection Officer
2. Lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commissioner of Malaysia
